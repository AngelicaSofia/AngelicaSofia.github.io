Publications

  • 2023.01.29
    Poli4sdg: An application for environmental crises management and gender support.
    International Journal of Computer and Information Engineering, 17(1):78–83, 2023
    This work presents POLI4SDG, a web and mobile prototype developed within the CROWD4SDG initiative to support SDG 13 (Climate Action). The application helps users access emergency services during environmental crises, integrating citizen science contributions and promoting inclusivity, particularly for vulnerable groups such as women.
  • 2021.10.07
    Teaching a Learner Driver using Reinforcement Learning and Planning Strategies
    Politecnico di Milano
    This work explores the application of Teacher-Student Reinforcement Learning in autonomous driving, focusing on racing scenarios. It investigates how a teacher can accelerate the learning process by providing guidance through planning-based optimal trajectories, both in offline and online settings.
  • 2021.09.06
    Exploring the runtime performance of knowledge graph embedding methods
    2021 IEEE 6th International Forum on Research and Technology for Society and Industry (RTSI), pages 463–468, 2021
    The work is a performance-focused evaluation of Knowledge Graph Embedding (KGE) models, introducing KGE-Perf, a framework for benchmarking memory and runtime efficiency. It highlights the impact of optimization strategies and shows GPU as the most effective architecture.
